Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375¬∞F (190¬∞C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter and both sugars until light and fluffy.
  4. Beat in eggs one at a time, then add the vanilla extract. Mix until well combined.
  5. Gradually blend in the flour mixture until just combined. Don't overmix!
  6. Stir in the chocolate chips and nuts (if using) by hand with a wooden spoon.
  7. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto the prepared baking sheets, spacing them about 2 inches apart.
  8. Bake for 9-11 minutes or until the edges are golden brown but centers still look slightly underbaked.
  9. Cool on baking sheets for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack.
  10. Enjoy! Store in an airtight container for up to one week.
